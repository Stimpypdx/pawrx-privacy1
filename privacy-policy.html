Privacy Policy for PawRx

Effective date: March 21, 2026

PawRx ("we", "us", or "our") operates the PawRx mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

PawRx is designed with your privacy in mind. All pet profiles, medication schedules, health records, and related data you enter into the App are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal identifying information.

In-App Purchases

PawRx offers optional premium features through in-app subscriptions. Purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store and by RevenueCat, our subscription management provider. We do not collect or store your payment information. RevenueCat may collect anonymized subscription status data to verify your premium access. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

Push Notifications

If you grant permission, PawRx sends local push notifications to remind you of medication schedules. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve transmitting any data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

PawRx does not use advertising networks, analytics platforms, or tracking tools. We do not share any data with third parties except as described in the In-App Purchases section above.

Children's Privacy

PawRx does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not target children and does not contain features designed to collect information from minors.

Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device, your information is protected by your device's own security features. We recommend using a passcode or biometric lock on your device to protect your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
